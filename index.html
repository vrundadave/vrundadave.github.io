About me

I received the B.Tech degree in Computer Science & engineering from Dharamsinh Desai Institute of Technology (DDIT), Gujarat, India, in 2014. Prior to joining PhD in CSE, IIT Bombay, I worked in Tata Research Development and Design Centre (TRDDC), Pune from Jun-Dec 2014. In January 2015, I started PhD in Theoretical Computer Science under the supervision of Prof. Krishna S.

Research

My research interests include

  • Automata, Logic and Transducers

  • Formal Verification

  • Rechability Games
